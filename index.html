TV shows of the Day

Bloodline

Bloodline is one of the many Netflix original gems that should be your next binge watch. The plot revolves around the Rayburn family, who are reminded of a tragic past when family member Danny returns home. A deep and complex plot unfolds that will keep you guessing and wanting more.

HBO's True Detective (Season 1)

The first season of True Detective goes down as my favorite season of a TV show of all time (season 2 lacked the intense plot that season 1 had). This dark crime drama has an intense plot and amazing character development that will lead you to being hooked instantly. Check out this show on HBOGO!
