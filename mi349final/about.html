About nickbunton.github.io

This website is about sharing my favorite media to anyone who wishes to visit this site. To make the most out of the site, I would suggest getting familiar with the layout.

(1) The Home page displays 2 songs, movies, and TV shows of the day. Each day of the week, 2 new songs, shows, and movies will be featured. You will be able to listen to songs and watch trailers via YouTube embedded videos (if there is no copyright privacy).

(2) Then there are 3 categories which you can explore more into (Music, TV, and Movies). Each of those pages will have a far more in depth selection and will be updated on a weekly basis.

(3) Each TV and Movie page will have ten featured items of the week, and then each week there will be 20 songs featured on the Music page. I felt as if music was better to have more of than TV and Movies because songs can be listening to quickly as opposed to the watching a movie or tv show. If you think I should have more featured items weekly, let me know!

(4) Finally, you are more than welcome to email me with feedback on how you feel about some of the media I post about, or if you think there is something I should post in the future. You can find my email at the bottom at any of the website pages.

And always, thanks for coming to nickbunton.github.io and hope to have you here again!
